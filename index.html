  • The Knights Hospitaller started in the early 12th century, in the kingdom of Jerusalem.


  • The knights Hospitaller started as a group who cared for sick and poor pilgrims in Jerusalem but started providing armed escorts until they became a significant military force.


  • The Hospitallers cared for pretty much anyone including Jews and Muslims. The patron saint for the Knights Hospitallers was Saint John the Baptist.


  • In 1565 the Great Siege of Malta occurred. The Ottoman Empire tried to conquer Malta.


  • The Knights Hospitaller kept them back. The siege lasted from 18 May to 12 September 1565.


  • In the end the Knights Hospitaller won and drove the Ottoman Empire out. It was one of the most well-known events at the time.
